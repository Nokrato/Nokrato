## About Me

I’m **Nokrato** — a builder focused on **modern web systems, workflow tools, dashboards, and automation**.

Most of what I work on sits at the intersection of **product thinking, technical structure, and execution**: turning messy ideas into usable systems that actually solve problems.

I’m especially interested in:
- full-stack web apps
- operational dashboards
- workflow automation
- AI-assisted tools
- system design
- UI structure that stays practical

---

## What I’m Building

- **business workflow systems** for logs, purchase orders, invoices, and operations
- **interactive floor plan and layout tools** with drag, align, spacing, and print-focused output
- **AI-assisted assistants and dashboards** for real business use
- **inventory, marketplace, and system-driven platforms** with structured data models
- **custom internal tools** designed to reduce manual work and speed up execution

## Build Approach

I prefer systems that are:
- **clean**
- **scalable**
- **useful**
- **fast to work with**
- **designed for real workflows, not just demos**

I care a lot about **structure, maintainability, and reducing friction**.  
If a process is repetitive, unclear, or slow, I want to fix it.
